INDIAN ELEPHANT

The Indian elephant(Elephas maximus indicus) is one of three recognised subspecies of the Asian elephant and native to mainland Asia. Since 1986, Elephants maximus has been listed as Endangered by IUCN as the population has declined by at least 50% over the last three generations, estimated to be 60-75 years. Asian elephants are threatened by habitat loss, degradation and frangmentation
